## Lighting Lessons Observed

- The repeated warning is that "电影感布光" by itself is too abstract for AI tools.
- The creator's examples convert filmic lighting into concrete prompt parts such as off-frame high side-back light, volumetric beams, and soft front fill.
- A hands-on correction example kept the image ratio unchanged, added cinematic lighting, and required natural, smooth light rays.
- The stronger result had a motivated side/back light, visible atmospheric beam, darker controlled shadows, and readable subject details.
- The broader prompt philosophy across the first visible topics is to make abstract taste words concrete: define structure, material, light, atmosphere, composition, and constraints.
